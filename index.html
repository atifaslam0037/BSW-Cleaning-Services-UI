Commercial Cleaning

Commercial cleaning involves the professional cleaning of commercial spaces, such as offices, retail stores, and other commercial properties. This type of cleaning is typically more thorough and extensive than regular cleaning, and requires specialized equipment and expertise to ensure a safe and hygienic environment for employees and customers.

Residential Cleaning

Residential cleaning refers to the cleaning of homes and living spaces, including apartments, condos, and houses. This type of cleaning can involve a variety of tasks, such as dusting, vacuuming, and sanitizing, and is typically done on a regular basis to maintain a clean and comfortable living environment.

Carpet & Floor Cleaning

Carpet and floor cleaning involves the professional cleaning of carpets and various types of flooring, including hardwood, tile, and vinyl. This type of cleaning can help remove dirt, stains, and allergens, and can prolong the life of carpets and flooring while maintaining a clean and hygienic environment.
